Announcing our new digital service platform

Author:
Digital Team
Published:
1 February 2026
Reading time:
5 minute read

Today marks an important milestone for our organisation as we launch our new digital service platform.

What's new

Our new platform has been designed with users at the heart of every decision. Based on extensive user research and feedback, we've made significant improvements to how people access our services.

Key improvements

  • Faster transactions - Average completion time reduced by 40%
  • Mobile-first design - Full functionality on all devices
  • Improved accessibility - Meeting WCAG 2.2 AA standards
  • Clearer guidance - Step-by-step help throughout the journey

User research insights

We conducted research with over 500 users during the development process. Key findings included:

  1. Users wanted clearer navigation and fewer steps
  2. Mobile access was increasingly important
  3. Progress saving was a frequently requested feature
  4. Plain English guidance was essential

"The new service is so much easier to use. I completed my application in half the time." — User research participant

What's next

This is just the beginning. Over the coming months, we'll be:

  • Adding new features based on user feedback
  • Expanding the range of services available online
  • Publishing performance data transparently
  • Continuously improving based on analytics

Get in touch

We'd love to hear your feedback on the new platform. You can:

  • Use the feedback link at the bottom of each page
  • Email us at
  • Follow our social media channels for updates

Thank you to everyone who participated in our research and testing. Your input has been invaluable in shaping this new service.
