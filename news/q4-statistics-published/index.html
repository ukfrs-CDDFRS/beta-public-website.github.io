Q4 2025 performance statistics published

Author:
Data Analysis Team
Published:
15 January 2026
Reading time:
3 minute read

We've published our performance statistics for Q4 2025, showing strong improvement across all key metrics.

Headline figures

Our services handled over 1.2 million transactions this quarter, with:

  • 92.1% user satisfaction (up from 90.0%)
  • 87.3% digital uptake (up from 83.0%)
  • £2.34 average cost per transaction (down from £2.55)

Monthly breakdown

Month Transactions Satisfaction Digital Uptake
October 2025 398,234 91.2% 86.1%
November 2025 412,890 92.5% 87.8%
December 2025 434,554 92.6% 88.0%

Analysis

The steady improvement reflects our ongoing investment in:

  1. User experience improvements - Simplified journeys and clearer guidance
  2. Performance optimization - Faster page loads and reduced errors
  3. Accessibility enhancements - Making services usable by everyone
  4. Support channel improvements - Better assisted digital support

Access the full data

You can explore the complete dataset on our Statistics Dashboard, which includes:

  • Downloadable data in CSV format
  • Interactive charts and visualizations
  • Historical comparisons
  • Service-by-service breakdowns

We remain committed to publishing this data quarterly as part of our transparency commitments.
